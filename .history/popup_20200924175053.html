Union Dental Holdings, Inc. engages in dental equipment, which develops, manufactures and markets atmospheric water generators for the dental market, home and office use. It provides dental networks for union members. The company was founded on November 26, 1996 and is headquartered in Coral Springs, FL.

Union Dental Holdings, Inc.. Union Dental Holdings, Inc. operates a dental practice in Coral Springs, Florida. The Company's subsidiary, Union Dental Corp. (UDC), operates a network of duly licensed dental providers providing dental services through the network to union members in accordance with arrangements between UDC and various unions. The Company has a joint venture agreement with Drinkable-Air Inc. for the research and development of a product called the Green Ozzonator. The UDC Dental Network consists of approximately 200 licensed dentists located in over 20 states. It maintains approximately 20 work stations, each with equipment to provide patients with a range of dental needs. Its Website is used as an informative site and dental directory for union patients in need of the services offered by the dentists in the network and to locate a network dentist. It owns approximately three digital X-Ray machines with platinum sensors, Gendex Panoram, cephologram and three dimensional (3-D) imaging equipment.
